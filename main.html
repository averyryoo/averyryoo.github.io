Hi! My name is

Avery (Hee-Woon) Ryoo

I am a Research Master's student at Mila and the Université de Montréal, where I study Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence. I am a member of the Sensory Integration and NeuroTheory Lab, where I am advised by Dr. Matthew Perich.

 

Before my graduate studies, I studied biomedical engineering at the University of Waterloo, during which I completed several internships in data science, computer vision research, and brain-computer interfaces.

 

Outside of research, I am passionate about the Toronto Raptors, professional soccer in Europe, and spicy food.
